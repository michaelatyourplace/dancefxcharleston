Quick Planning Tip

Pick one “anchor activity” (a class), then build the rest around dinner and a simple night-out route. A class gives your group something to do together—no awkward downtime.

Why classes are perfect for a girls night out

Classes make it easy to keep everyone engaged—especially when your group has different vibes. They’re fun, structured, and usually come with plenty of moments for photos, laughs, and bonding. FAQ: How far in advance should we book?

For weekends in spring and summer, try to book at least 2–4 weeks ahead—especially for private group times. If your group is flexible, weekday evenings often have better availability.

FAQ: What size groups work best?

Most class-based ideas work great for 6–14 people. For larger groups, ask about splitting into two sessions or booking a private event slot.
